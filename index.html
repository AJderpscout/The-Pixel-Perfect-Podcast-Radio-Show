Chaffey College 1630AM Radio Show Schedule

Welcome to The Pixel Perfect Podcast! This radio show is all about videogames. Specifically videogame music and the lesser known gems in the modern gaming space. On some days, reviews about these gems will be given, spoiler free of course. News about the everchanging landscape that is the gaming industry will also be discussed. On others we will be taking a look at the music behind games and enjoying said music.

Live Every Thursday At: 11a.m. PST
